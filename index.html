how to use

Combine Sheets is easy tool to combine multiple worksheets into one. Follow the source format or Append to existing or remove Duplicates. Everything on a click..


Go to "Home" tab on MS Excel and click "Combine Sheets" icon to load..


1.>>Hit Get Started!



2.>>Choose the Worksheets to Combine and hit "Combine Sheets" Button

3.>>More Options under "Settings" icon

Troubleshoot

  • Add-in not loading? :

    1.Clear Browsing History and retry. Un-Install And Re-Install Addin.

    2.Ensure That You Are Using Chrome Or Edge Browser Or Try Both .

    2.Enable Javascript In Browser.


    Add-On showing old version on Desktop or Web ? : Clear the Office Cache.

    Clear the Office cache and try again.
